How to Measure Ring Size

  • Wrap a string around your finger at the base
  • Mark where the string overlaps
  • Measure the length in millimeters
  • Use the circumference column to find your size
  • Measure at room temperature for accuracy

How to Measure Bangle Size

  • Close your fingers together and bring your thumb to your little finger
  • Measure across the widest part of your hand
  • Add 1-2mm for comfortable fit
  • For thick bangles, add 2-3mm extra space
  • Traditional bangles should fit snugly
