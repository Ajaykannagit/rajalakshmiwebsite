How to care for my jewelry?

Proper care ensures your jewelry maintains its beauty and lasts for generations. Here are our expert recommendations:

Daily Care:

  • Remove jewelry before bathing, swimming, or exercising
  • Avoid contact with perfumes, lotions, and chemicals
  • Clean with soft, dry cloth after each wear
  • Store pieces separately to prevent scratching

Deep Cleaning:

  • Use warm soapy water and soft brush for gold
  • Professional cleaning every 6 months recommended
  • Avoid harsh chemicals and ultrasonic cleaners for delicate pieces

For detailed care instructions, visit our Jewelry Care Guide.

How often should I get my jewelry professionally cleaned?

The frequency depends on how often you wear the jewelry and the type of piece:

Recommended Schedule:

  • Daily wear jewelry: Every 3-4 months
  • Occasional wear pieces: Every 6-8 months
  • Special occasion jewelry: Once a year
  • Antique pieces: As needed, with special care

Signs you need professional cleaning: Dullness, visible dirt buildup, loose stones, or damaged clasps.

How can I verify the authenticity of my jewelry?

We provide multiple ways to verify the authenticity of your jewelry purchases:

Verification Methods:

  • BIS Hallmark: Check for official BIS marking on gold jewelry
  • Certificate Verification: Verify certificates online through issuing authority
  • Store Verification: Bring jewelry to our store for free authentication
  • Third-party Testing: Independent assay center verification

What to Look For:

  • BIS logo and purity number (916, 750, etc.)
  • Jeweler's identification mark
  • Assay center code
  • Year of hallmarking
